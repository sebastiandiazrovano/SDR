Sebastián Díaz Rovano

Sebastian Diaz (b. 1978, Santiago de Chile) is a Berlin-based interdisciplinary artist who works primarily in painting, video and performance. His work deals with family intimacy and its traumas, as well as everyday scenes whose mystery holds a critical view of our relationship with the environment and the collective..
